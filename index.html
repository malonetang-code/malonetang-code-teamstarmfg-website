40+ Years of Craftsmanship Heritage

伟群制刀工业股份有限公司在台湾已有40多年的历史，是专业从事生产各项产业之工业刀具的公司。1990年在深圳创立伟群精密机械制品有限公司，在中国也有30多年的生产经验。

Wei Qun Cutting Tools has over 40 years of history in Taiwan as a professional manufacturer of industrial cutting tools. Established Shenzhen Wei Qun in 1990 with over 30 years of production experience in China.

公司集研发、自主设计、制造于一体，致力于成为国内外客户最信赖的工业刀具伙伴。

Integrating R&D, design, and manufacturing to be the most trusted industrial cutting tool partner for customers worldwide.
